Ooty

Perched at 2,240 metres in the Nilgiri mountains, Ooty is a colonial hill station known for its sprawling tea gardens, stunning viewpoints, and pleasant climate year-round. Experience the charm of the Botanical Garden, the panoramic views from Doddabetta Peak, serene Ooty Lake, and the fragrant Rose Garden.

Botanical Garden Doddabetta Peak Ooty Lake Rose Garden Tea Factory
Explore Ooty Packages →

Coonoor

The quieter, more serene cousin of Ooty, Coonoor offers hidden waterfalls, the iconic Nilgiri Mountain Railway, and pristine tea estates untouched by mass tourism. Walk through Sim's Park, gaze from Dolphin's Nose viewpoint, and explore the ruins of Droog Fort.

Sim's Park Dolphin's Nose Lamb's Rock Droog Fort Hidden Falls
Explore Coonoor Packages →

Palakkad

Known as the gateway to Kerala through the Palakkad Gap, this district offers a blend of history, nature, and culture. Visit the 18th-century Palakkad Fort, explore Silent Valley National Park, and enjoy the tranquil Malampuzha Dam and its beautiful gardens.

Palakkad Fort Silent Valley Malampuzha Dam Jain Temple
Explore Palakkad Packages →

Coimbatore Stops

On the route from Bangalore to the Nilgiris, Coimbatore offers its own cultural gems worth exploring. Visit the ancient Marudhamalai Temple, relax at VOC Park, or cool off at Kovai Kutralam Falls before heading up to the hills.

Marudhamalai Temple VOC Park Kovai Kutralam
