Discover South India

Ooty — Queen of Hills

Sprawling tea gardens, misty viewpoints, and colonial charm nestled at 2,240 metres. Explore the Botanical Garden, Doddabetta Peak, Ooty Lake, Rose Garden, and the famous Tea Factory.

Coonoor — Hidden Paradise

Hidden waterfalls, the iconic Nilgiri Mountain Railway, and serene tea estates away from the tourist crowds. Discover Sim's Park, Dolphin's Nose, Lamb's Rock, and Droog Fort.

Palakkad — Gateway to Kerala

Ancient forts, lush paddy fields, and the majestic Silent Valley. Experience authentic Kerala culture, cuisine, Malampuzha Dam, and the serene Jain Temple.
